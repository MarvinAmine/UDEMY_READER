Udemy Quiz Reader

Read Udemy AWS certification questions and answers directly on the page.

  1. Open a Udemy practice exam / quiz.
  2. Under the question, look for the “Quiz Reader” bar.
  3. Click “▶ Play Q + answers” to start reading.
  4. Highlight will follow the text while it’s being read.
  5. Use Pause / Resume / Stop as needed.
